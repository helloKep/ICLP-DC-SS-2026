\section{Research goals}\label{sec:goal}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item The goal of this research is to establish best practices for modeling large railway systems in answer set programming
	\item Our first goal has been to understand large railway systems by leveraging the virtual railway environment generator, Flatland, and developing a comprehensive formalization of the problem as well as initial encodings to see how this problem differs from those in similar domains
	\item Specific potential implementations of this research include:
	\begin{enumerate}
		\item Routing and scheduling problems
		\item Acute replanning during adverse events
		\item Valid switch position logic (ILTIS system)
		\item Energy utilization
		\item Route synchronization
	\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\section{Goal of the research}\label{sec:goal}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item The goal of this research is to establish practices for utilizing answer set programming to address the issues faced in railway operations
	\item Specific potential implementations of this research include:
	\begin{enumerate}
		\item Routing and scheduling problems
		\item Acute replanning during adverse events
		\item Valid switch position logic (ILTIS system)
		\item Energy utilization
		\item Route synchronization
	\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
